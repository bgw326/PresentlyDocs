# Support – Presently

Thank you for using Presently!

If you’re experiencing issues or have questions, this page explains how to get help, report bugs, and request data-related actions.

## Contact Us
- Email: sleeker_amphora.0b@icloud.com 
- Response Time: I aim to respond within as soon as I'm able. 

When contacting me for support, please include:
- A short description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce (if applicable)
- Your device model (e.g., iPhone 15)
- Your iOS version (e.g., iOS 18.1)
- App version (from Presently’s Settings, if available)

## Troubleshooting
Try these steps before contacting support:
1. Make sure you’re on the latest version of Presently from the App Store.
2. Force-quit the app and reopen it.
3. Restart your device.
4. If the issue persists, send us a detailed report (see “Contact Us”).

## Background Refresh & Updates
Presently may refresh data in the background to keep content up to date. If you notice stale data:
- Ensure Background App Refresh is enabled in iOS Settings.
- Open the app to trigger a manual refresh.
- If issues persist, contact us with details.

## Known Issues
- If you encounter any unexpected behavior (e.g., missing updates, delays), please report it. We actively track and fix problems based on your feedback.

## Feature Requests
I welcome suggestions! Open a GitHub issue with the label “feature request” or email me with:
- A clear description of the feature
- Why it’s helpful
- Any examples or screenshots (optional)
- Tell me if you supported me as a paid user or are just using the free features. 

## Data & Privacy
- There is no need to request data deletion. the Presently app does not collect any data and all data is stored and controlled by your device

## Subscriptions & Billing
If you subscribed through the App Store:
- Manage or cancel your subscription via: iOS Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions.
- For billing issues, Apple Support may be able to assist directly.

If you need help understanding your subscription in Presently, contact us with your Apple ID’s masked receipt details (no sensitive information, please).

## Reporting Bugs
Open a GitHub issue or email us. For faster resolution, include:
- Steps to reproduce
- What you expected to happen
- What actually happened
- Screenshots or screen recordings (if possible)
- Device, iOS version, and app version
