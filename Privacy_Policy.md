# Privacy Policy for Presently

**Last Updated: November 26, 2025**

## Introduction

Presently ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Presently iOS application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Calendar Data
- The App accesses your device's calendar data to display your events
- **All calendar data stays on your device** - we do not collect, store, or transmit your calendar information to any servers
- Calendar access requires your explicit permission through iOS system prompts

### Purchase Information
- When you make in-app purchases, Apple processes the transaction
- We receive only confirmation that a purchase was made (no payment details)
- Apple handles all payment processing according to their privacy policy

### Data We Do NOT Collect
We do not collect, store, or share:
- Personal identification information
- Contact information
- Location data
- Usage analytics
- Calendar event details
- Any other personal data

## How We Use Information

The App uses your calendar data solely to:
- Display your events in the app interface
- Calculate time until upcoming events
- Show countdowns and notifications you configure
- Provide Live Activity widgets with event information

All processing happens locally on your device.

## Data Storage

- All app data and preferences are stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults, local database)
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- Subscription status is managed by Apple through StoreKit

## Third-Party Services

### Apple StoreKit
- Used for in-app purchase processing
- Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

### iOS Calendar Access
- The App uses iOS EventKit framework to access calendar data
- You control calendar access through iOS Settings → Presently → Calendars

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is not directed at children.

## Data Security

- All data remains on your device
- Calendar access is protected by iOS system permissions
- We employ standard iOS security practices

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Revoke calendar access** at any time through iOS Settings
- **Delete the App** which removes all local app data
- **Request information** about data handling by contacting us
- **Manage subscriptions** through your App Store account settings

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## International Users

The App is designed for worldwide use. By using the App, you consent to the processing of your data as described in this policy.
